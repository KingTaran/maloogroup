Background Matters!

Maloo Organisation’s presence in central India can be traced back to over five decades when in the year 1969 it took shape in the form of a Pharmacy by the name of “M/s. Jain Medical Store” / “M/s. Maloo Medical Store” at Mahasamund in the erstwhile state of Madhya Pradesh.

With the dint of sheer hard work, vision and self-belief Mr. Ashok Maloo laid the foundations of the Maloo Group of Organisations as we see them today. Hard work never goes waste and so it was in his case too; his hard work paid abundant returns and rich dividends and he subsequently moved the business to Raipur and started M/s. Maloo Pharmaceuticals, a business model based on distribution and wholesale of pharmaceutical products. He focused on the finer aspects of distribution like market coverage and serviced with great fervour the interiors and micro interiors of Chhattisgarh.

Chhattisgarh is truly a land of opportunities. It is one of the richest States in mineral resources, culminating into Steel, Aluminum and Cement industries and abundant Power Production. And Raipur as the capital of Chhattisgarh, has witnessed and is still witnessing major growth in many sectors such as the real estate, manufacturing, trading and education to name just a few. Under the circumstances, with an ever burgeoning population, it is but natural that the medical sector wouldn’t be far behind.

The Organisation is now in the able hands of the leading next generation entrepreneur Mr. Amit Maloo who has imbibed the core business values from his father and brought in his own skills and personal capabilities to good effect thus leading a team that works. MGO believes in training, delegating and coordinating; its personal management style has been to invest in human assets and empower the employees with all the necessary tools, training and development that are perennially essential to help them perform and achieve companywide objectives along with synchronous personal and individual objectives of the people themselves.
